Convenient for car refills
This sturdy jug is perfect to keep in the car for refills and thirsty moments. It replaced my previous one that lasted 5 years of daily hard use. I prefer using this BPA-free jug over disposable water bottles because it allows me to control the quality of my intake. Cheers to good health!
Great for my needs
This water jug is the perfect size for me to bring extra water with me when I go out for the day. So far, it hasn't tipped over in my truck, but I can't say for sure how leak proof the cap is since some other reviews have mentioned a potential issue with the seal. It's important to use common sense and handle it properly - these jugs are not indestructible like some people may expect them to be. I recommend carrying it by the handle rather than using the retention strap for the cap. If you accidentally drop it while there's water inside, there's a chance that it could crack. It's important to have reasonable expectations. If you're looking for a rugged water jug, you might want to consider something other than a plastic one that costs less than $20. But personally, this one works perfectly fine for me.
Perfect Gallon
I really like this gallon! It functions effectively and is leak-proof and lightweight. It's quite durable, although it may break if dropped with water inside due to its lightness. While a metal gallon might be more sturdy, I prefer the lighter option as someone who travels frequently.
